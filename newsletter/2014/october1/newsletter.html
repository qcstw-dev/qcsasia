The PSS line is the plastic version of the zamac ZSS line of keychain with
rubber loop. Push down the loop to open it and release a key, pull back
to close the loop and fix the keychain. It comes with a recess on 1 side
or both sides which fit standard (#ODD205) 20 mm size doming.

Easy to use and functional, this promotional product is light and soft,
it won’t scratch your car’s door or your mobile phone in your pocket.
