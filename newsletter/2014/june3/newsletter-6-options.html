- QCS produces soft PVC promotional products since more than 20 years now

- We only use first grade imported PVC raw material from legitimate and large petrochemical suppliers.

- All raw material are EN71 compliant including low cadmium, AZO free…etc.

Production process:

- Soft pvc item are produced the same way as enamel product do. The liquid pvc color is hand filled with a syringe inside the tooling cavity.

- Each color is separated by the carving inside the tooling, hence the word “cloisonne”, which refer to “cloison” ( “wall” in French ), original calling for hand color fill enamel process.
