Custom medal and recognition awards can come with many style and type, from cheap promotional products, usually aluminium offset printing, to deluxe gifts and exclusive item in zamac (zinc alloy) with hard enamel and antic finish.

Custom Medal and award can also be made with a metal base, usually iron or zamac, with doming custom branded part. Metal base with doming is usually for stockers so the metal can be made in high volume cost efficiency and then custom in very small quantity, and rush delivery, with a made to order full color printing dome.

Medal and award come with a large array of attachment and accessories, from lanyard, ribbon, brooch, wood frame and base.
